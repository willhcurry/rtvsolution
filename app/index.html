Returns Handling

  • Refurbished products are processed to return to resalable open box or b-stock quality. These products can be used for warranty coverage keeping your new stock untouched for new sales orders. Additionally, refurbished products can be sold on the secondary market introduced in a carefully managed way to control the price of this market and ultimately the price of your A (new) stock. We follow the price of your A-Stock to prevent overlap which would otherwise result in pricing issues. This ensures the protection of your main business price.
